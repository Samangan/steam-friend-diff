What is This?

I love Steam, but I have a problem that I run into very often. I never know what games to play with my friends. I spend hours pouring over our steam game libraries trying to find games we both play. I made this tool to make it easier to see what games we could play now and in the future. It is still very early in development and many more features are going to be added.

If you are interested in seeing more please fill in the links to the steam profiles you want to diff.
